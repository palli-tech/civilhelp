Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

CivilHelp Workforce Management ("CivilHelp", "we", "our", or "us") provides workforce management software for contractors, businesses, and workforce administrators.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and protected when using the CivilHelp Workforce Management application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • User role and permissions

Workforce Information

  • Worker names
  • Worker contact information
  • Worker information entered by authorized administrators

Attendance Information

  • Attendance records
  • Work dates
  • Site assignments
  • Overtime information

Payroll and Payment Information

  • Wage rates
  • Payroll calculations
  • Advance payments
  • Payment records
  • Outstanding balances

How We Use Information

  • Provide workforce management services
  • Manage workers and attendance
  • Calculate payroll and payments
  • Generate reports
  • Maintain account security
  • Improve application reliability and performance

Data Storage

Information is stored using secure cloud infrastructure and databases managed by trusted service providers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared only with authorized users of the same organization, service providers required to operate the application, or when required by law.

Security

We implement reasonable safeguards designed to protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Children's Privacy

CivilHelp Workforce Management is intended for business use and is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updated versions will be posted on this page.
